Meet Pastor ‘Daniel
Home | Meet Pastor ‘Daniel

Pastor Bosun Ola Daniel popularly known as BOD is a teacher, prophet, Evangelist and inspirational speaker, whose life has been a blessing and inspiration to so many lives and shall continue to be at home and abroad.

He is the founder and general overseer of Watchtower Christian Church of God int'l.An Apostolic Ministry dedicated to the preparation of the soul of man for the second coming of our Lord Jesus Christ by giving them sweetness from the infallible Word of God and eradicating all forms of bitterness in people's lives.

Pastor Bosun is happily married to Pastor (Mrs) Oluwafunmilayo Bosun and blessed with two beautiful daughters - Precious and Peculiar
